About us

Hope Supermarket started business at Busiika in 2014 by ladies, Christian Believers of the Seventh Day Adventist faith. The start was not an easy one, but Hope kept us going. We find Hope a better name for our company because of the Hope we have in the second coming of our Lord Jesus Christ.

'God cares is Our moto'


Other services

Apart from the products we have in stock, Hope supermarket offers a wide range of non-business activities to both the church and community, free ofcharge.

  • We offer coference space for discusion on urgent issues for church leaders in Bugema B District
  • Hope Supermarket is a payment and collection center for quarterly devotional books for christians such us
    1. Adult Lesson study guides
    2. Children's study guides for Beginner, Primary and Kindergarten classes
    3. Youths study guides
    4. Morning watch or daily devotional readings for the christian home
    5. Hymn books and Bibles
  • Our neighbours are able to subsidise their cost for parking fees as they use our parking space free of charge when we close business on the Sabbath
